Reclaim

A 3D walkthrough experience created as part of my fourth year university Honours Project. Its aim is to showcase anthropological environmental destruction and flora regrowth within a contained scene.

The project features two distinct timelines - the first one being at the peak of human activity and the second one after a theoretical disappearance of humans, where nature has had the chance to regrow.

Software used:

  • ZBrush
  • Maya
  • Substance Painter
  • Substance Designer
  • Photoshop
  • SpeedTree
  • Unreal Engine

Date: 2020-2021
